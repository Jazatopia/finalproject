About Me:


For more than 20 years Ms. Lawson aka Jazatopia has showcased her talent as an entertainer. From a dancer to a songwriter, arranger, producer and singer. Lets just say entertainment is in her blood. Whether creating melodies and visual projects for others or herself she is always in Re-invention mode. Jazatopia started off training to be a dancer in high school in her hometown of Detroit, MI. After graduation she performed locally in hair shows, with a rap artist and as a featured dancer on WADLs Dance Party. She also has been arranging and producing music under Snowball Records (Indie) label. She worked with a couple of artists in her hometown of Detroit, MI and has decided to put out music for herself. She has produced and arranged Symptoms of Love by Lois, Get to Steppin by Grady Washington and Baddest Chick by Cientell, as well as others. Her debut album Man Up is available on Digital Download on CD Baby, iTunes, Amazon, Google Play, etc. Her music is a mixture of R and B and EDM. Jazatopias musical influences are; Janet Jackson, Prince, Missy Elliot, Timbaland, Pharrell, Kayne West, Jodeci, R. Kelly, Brandy.
Her current influence is Dawn Richard.
The Re-invention continues ever evolving into greatness!
